Auto-detected ticker renames

When a symbol disappears between IBKR snapshots and another symbol appears with matching quantity (after accounting for trades, transfers, splits, and explicit corporate actions), the matcher treats it as a non-taxable rollover so the basis follows the security through bankruptcy / CUSIP changes / etc.

Common pattern: a stock entering Chapter 11 gets a "Q" suffix (e.g. ABC → ABCQ), or is renamed entirely after an FDIC takeover or multi-step bankruptcy reorganization (e.g. ABC → ABCQ → XYZ).
