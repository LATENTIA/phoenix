Rules applied (Belgium, KPMG July 2025 draft):
  • Flat 10% rate on net realized gains (gains − losses) per calendar year.
  • Annual exemption: 10,000 EUR per taxpayer. Up to 1,000 EUR/year of unused exemption rolls forward, banked balance capped at 15,000 EUR.
  • Pre-2026 lots: cost basis is reset to the 2025-12-31 close or the original buy basis if it is higher (favorable for 5 years).
  • Losses offset gains within the same year only; no carry-forward of losses.
  • All gains realized on or before 2025-12-31 are exempt under the transitional rule.
  • Ticker renames auto-detected from snapshot reconciliation (e.g. a stock entering Chapter 11 and getting a "Q" suffix). Cost basis rolls forward through these renames so the loss is claimed on the eventual close. See the Detected renames tab to audit each one.
